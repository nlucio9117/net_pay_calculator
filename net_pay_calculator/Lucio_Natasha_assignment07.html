Calculating Net Pay

This page will calculate an employee's net pay. Begin process by clicking on the 'BEGIN' button below.

The user will be prompted for:

  • Employee's hourly rate of pay;
  • Number of hours worked that week; and
  • Number of dependents employee has.

The user can change the background color selected from:

  • red
  • pink
  • green
  • yellow
  • lavender
